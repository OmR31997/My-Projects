About Our Application

Welcome to our Movies and Shows Library application!

This app lets you explore a wide range of movies and shows categorized by:

  • Genre: Action, Drama, Comedy, Animation, and more.
  • Country: USA, India, Japan, etc.
  • Language: English, Hindi, Spanish, and others.

Our app provides detailed information, including titles, release dates, ratings, and trailers, to enhance your viewing experience.

Features:

  • Search and filter movies by categories and regions.
  • Save your favorites in watchlists.
  • View detailed information about each movie/show.
